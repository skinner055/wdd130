It does help me learn to be independent and figuring things on my own which is an important skill if others don't take the work seriously. I had a good experience as a Jr. Dev. But unfortunately my Senior Dev experience and client experience was lackluster. Overall though, I do see the potential for this assignment but it is very important for communication and participation to be included.
