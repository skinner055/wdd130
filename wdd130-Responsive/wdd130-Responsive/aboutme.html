About Brother Alvey

It all began one bright and lovely Sunday morning at the very start of spring in the year of our Lord, 1978. On May 21st a beautiful baby boy was born at the Primary Children's Hospital in Salt Lake City, Utah. No cuter baby had ever been born, all who saw him loved him, and he was name Zachariah Joseph Alvey!

It is now 2025 and his hair is gray and his cheeks are just as chubby as his first day on earth. He has grown to a man, a husband, and father. He now has 8 children of his own and he is teaching a bunch of misfit students how to program webpages using HTML and CSS. He live is straight out of a story book and everyone still loves him and his name is now Brother Alvey!
